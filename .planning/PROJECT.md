# ChatBot Pro

## Problem
SMBs lose potential customers outside business hours because they can't afford 24/7 human support. Questions go unanswered, leads go cold, and competitors who respond faster win the business.

## Solution
An AI chatbot trained on your business docs and FAQs that lives on your website, answers customer questions instantly around the clock, and smoothly hands off to humans when needed.

## Target User
- **Industry:** Service-based SMBs (agencies, consultants, local services)
- **Company size:** 10-50 employees
- **Role:** Founder, operations manager, or customer success lead
- **Pain:** Losing leads after hours, drowning in repetitive questions
- **Budget:** $29-99/month (Intercom alternative positioning)
- **Technical skill:** Non-technical, needs no-code setup

## Value Proposition
Train an AI on your business in minutes. Answer customer questions 24/7. Never miss a lead again.
